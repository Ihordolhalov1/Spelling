SW – Spelling Words

A simple spelling game for kids and parents

At my daughter’s school, children are given weekly spelling homework — usually a list of 10 words to memorise.

I noticed that, like many modern children, she learns spelling much better through games on a device, especially while we spend up to 30 minutes a day driving to school.

Existing apps don’t allow adding all custom words, so I built SW – Spelling Words myself.
